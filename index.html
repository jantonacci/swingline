Swingline helps you spend less time on log search and more time on log review. Storage performance issues require reviewing multiple ESXi host's log bundles, correlating events, and educating both customer and vendors. We need an automated method to accelerate review, generate results suitable for collaboration, and do quick statistical analysis. Swingline!
